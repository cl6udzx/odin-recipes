Ingredientes

  • 1 - Medium onion
  • 1 - Garlic
  • 1 c. of soup - Tomato paste
  • 400 g - Long grain rice
  • 1 q.b - Black pepper
  • 1 q.b - Paprika
  • 1.5L - Water
  • 100 ml - Red wine
  • 150g - Meat sausage
  • 150g - Smoked bacon
  • 4 - duck breast
  • 1 c. of tea - Salt
  • 50 ml - Olive oil

Preparation

  1. In a saucepan, place the liter and a half of water, the red wine, half the chorizo, the smoked bacon, the bay leaves, the duck breasts and the salt.
  2. Let it cook for 40 minutes.
  3. Strain and reserve the duck cooking broth.
  4. In a saucepan, sauté with olive oil, chopped onion and chopped garlic. Let it brown for 3 minutes and add the tomato pulp.
  5. Let it settle for 5 minutes.
  6. Let the meat cool down, shred the duck and cut the chorizo ​​and smoked bacon into cubes. Add to the braised pan.
  7. Add a little broth and let it simmer for 3 minutes.
  8. Add the rice and mix well. Then add 400 ml of the cooking broth, pepper and paprika. When it boils, lower the heat and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  9. Place the rice on a baking tray. Cut the remaining chorizo ​​into slices and decorate over the rice.
  10. Bake at 170ºC for 15 minutes until golden brown.
