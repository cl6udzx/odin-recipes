  • 1 - Hotdog sausage

Sauce

The sauce is a personal choice.

Preparation

  1. Toast the bread slices. Cut the sausage in half and lengthwise.
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper, place the pan on the heat with the butter and fry the steak on both sides. Reserve.
  3. Assemble the francesinha in the following order: slice of bread, ham, sausage, steak, slice of bread and cheese covering the francesinha. Reserve.
  4. Sauté the chopped onion in olive oil, add the chopped garlic, chilli pepper and bay leaf.
  5. Add the tomato, beer, wine, water, Worcestershire sauce and meat broth. Let it boil for 10 minutes. Season with salt.
  6. Add cornstarch diluted in cold water and mix. Finally, add the port wine and boil for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and grind with the magic wand.
  7. Pour 3 tablespoons of the sauce over the francesinha and brown it in the oven at 180ºC on the grill function for 5 minutes.
  8. Pour the remaining sauce over the francesinha.
  9. Fry the egg in a frying pan with the olive oil and place it on top of the francesinha.
