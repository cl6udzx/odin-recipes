Ingredientes

  • 200g - Beef to cook
  • 200g - Pig shovel
  • 200g - Chicken meat
  • 1 - Black pudding
  • 150g - Smoked bacon
  • 1 - Meat sausage
  • 400g - Savoy cabbage
  • 1 - Leek
  • 4 - Potato
  • 2 - Turnip
  • 4 - Carrot
  • 3 - Garlic

Preparation

  1. Wash the meats well and place them in a pressure cooker to cook for 25 minutes. Once cooked, reserve the meat and broth.
  2. Wash and cut the cabbage and leek into pieces. Wash, peel and halve the potatoes, turnips, carrots and garlic. Then place them in a pan.
  3. Add the previously cooked meats and cover up to half with water. The other half, with the meat cooking broth.
  4. Cook covered for 15 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked.
  5. Remove the meats and cut them into pieces.
  6. Place the meats and vegetables on a wide platter and serve.
  7. I suggest you serve it with white rice cooked in the remaining meat cooking water.
